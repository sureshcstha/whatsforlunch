About

Hi, Welcome to What's for Lunch?


This website is a lunch roulette helping you decide where to go for lunch.


Oftentimes we come into situations where we can’t decide what to eat and end up eating the same food every day. There are a lot of choices in our everyday life. For some people, lots of choices bring anxiety. So, I built this website to help decide where to eat. The purpose of this website is to bring some fun and adventure in where we choose to eat. This can be a decision making tool when we are in a group of people (especially office staff members who go lunch together) and we don’t want to go with the majority all the time.
